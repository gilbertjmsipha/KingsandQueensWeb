About Us

Kings and Queens Real Funeral Services is a distinguished funeral service provider operating in Zimbabwe and South Africa. Established in South Africa in 2004, the company expanded its services to Zimbabwe in 2010.

Our mission is to exceed customer expectations by providing memorable and dignified funeral services. We aim to deliver excellence, strive for continuous improvement, and respond promptly to change, while nurturing and promoting wholesome cultural and religious values.
