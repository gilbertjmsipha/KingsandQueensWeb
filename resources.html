Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

❓

What documents do I need to arrange a funeral?

📌

You will need:
• Death certificate
• National ID of the deceased
• Burial order (for Zimbabwe)
• Import/export permit (for repatriation)

❓

How long does it take to process funeral arrangements?

📌

Depending on the services chosen, it can take 24 hours to a few days. We offer same-day services for urgent cases.

❓

Does Kings & Queens offer payment plans?

📌

Yes, we offer flexible funeral insurance and installment plans to ensure affordability.

❓

Can I pre-plan my own funeral?

📌

Yes! Pre-planning allows you to arrange everything in advance and relieve your family of financial stress.

❓

How does repatriation work?

📌

We handle all paperwork, preservation, and transportation to return your loved one home with dignity.
